Joyce Nelson

Joyce Nelson is an award-winning freelance writer/researcher whose sixth book is being published in October 2016. Entitled Beyond Banksters: Resisting the New Feudalism, the book can be ordered at http://watershedsentinel.ca/banksters now.

Nelson writes regularly for The Watershed Sentinel, Counterpunch, Economic Reform and other publications. She is also a visual artist whose work is regularly exhibited by the Beach Guild of Fine Art in Toronto, Canada.

Nelson can be reached at nelsonbeach9@gmail.com.
